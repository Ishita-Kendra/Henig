Platform Recommendation
Zakeke vs GlamAR — which fits Henig?

Two strong platforms, different strengths. For a $3,500 engagement ring sold purely online, the choice matters.

Primary recommendation
GlamAR
Augmented reality try-on via phone camera — built for jewellery
✓ Customer holds up their phone and sees the ring on their actual hand in real time
✓ No app download required — runs in the browser directly on henigdiamonds.com
✓ Built specifically for rings, necklaces, and earrings — uses precise finger and wrist detection
✓ Fine jewellery retailers report 32.7% add-to-cart lift and 17.4% return reduction (GlamAR, 2024)
✓ High visual accuracy — diamond sparkle and metal finish rendered correctly on the customer's skin tone
✗ Less suited for showing real-time product customisation options
Why GlamAR for Henig: A $3,500 engagement ring is an emotional decision. The single biggest purchase barrier is "I don't know how it will look on me." GlamAR removes that barrier before the customer opens a competitor tab.
Strong Year 2 addition
Zakeke
3D product visualisation + customisation — broad e-commerce platform
✓ 3D rotate and zoom on every product — customers inspect from all angles on screen
✓ Real-time customisation (metal colour, stone, engraving) with a live 3D preview
✓ Strong integration with custom websites and all major e-commerce platforms
✓ Reports up to 250% conversion lift for jewellery (including 3D viewer + configurator combined)
✗ 3D viewer on screen — not live camera AR. Customer sees the product but not on their own hand
✗ More complex to implement across a large catalogue
Best if: Henig expands into bespoke or customisable pieces where the customer configures a ring before buying. An excellent Year 2 layer on top of GlamAR.

Why Act Now
📈
58% YoY growth in 2024
Jewellery brands using AR try-on grew 58% year-on-year in 2024–2025. Early adopters are building audience trust, search authority, and brand differentiation that latecomers will have to buy at much higher cost.
📱
Two thirds prefer it over a store visit
66% of luxury shoppers would rather use AR try-on than travel to a physical store. For a brand selling exclusively online via henigdiamonds.com, AR is the equivalent of opening a store — at a fraction of the cost.
💎
Highest impact at $3,500 ASP
The higher the price, the bigger the purchase anxiety. At $3,500 per piece, even a 35% improvement in conversion creates hundreds of thousands in additional revenue. AR directly addresses the one question holding customers back: how does it look on me?
