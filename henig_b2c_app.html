Methodology & footnotes
1
Opportunity score is a weighted composite (out of 10) across six criteria: market size/TAM (25%), ASP alignment with Henig's product range (20%), Henig credential fit — GIA/IGI, KP, RJC (20%), market growth rate 2024–25 (15%), ease of market entry (10%), and margin potential (10%). Source: Henig internal scoring model based on Bain, Statista, Grand View Research data.
2
ROI — TAM capture estimates Henig's realistic first-year revenue by applying a score-weighted capture rate (0.08% of TAM per score point) to the 2025 market size. Gross profit is then calculated at the selected margin %, and ROI = (gross profit − annual promo spend) ÷ annual promo spend. This method is conservative and market-size sensitive — larger markets yield higher absolute returns even at low capture rates.
3
ROI — units × margin estimates revenue from the bottom up: a score-weighted unit capture rate (0.12% of available unit pool) is applied to each market's estimated unit volume, multiplied by the segment ASP, then gross-margined. ROI is calculated identically to method 1. This method is more sensitive to ASP and unit availability, making it a better proxy for Henig's actual sales pipeline potential.
4
Monthly promo budget ranges represent estimated all-in digital marketing spend (paid search, Meta/Instagram, influencer fees, and trade event allowance) based on Henig's Shopify Promotion Plan benchmarks. Conservative = low-end of range; Aggressive = high-end; Moderate = midpoint. Actual spend may vary by channel mix and seasonality.
5
Gross margin slider applies a single blended gross margin assumption across all markets for comparability. Henig's actual margin will vary by product category (LGD vs natural diamond), channel (DTC vs wholesale), and geography. Recommended starting assumption: 38–45% for mid-luxury, 45–60% for luxury.
6
Data sources: Market size and growth figures — Bain & Company Altagamma 2024, Grand View Research 2025, Cognitive Market Research, Statista Diamond Jewellery Report 2025. ESG and consumer trend data — The Plumb Club 2025, IndexBox Ireland 2025. Trade show recommendations — JA New York, Inhorgenta Munich, Couture Las Vegas show directories.
